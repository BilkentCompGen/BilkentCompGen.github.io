Bioinformatics & Computational Genomics

Alkan Lab - September 2021. Standing: Malavi, Sami, Zülal, Ricardo. Sitting: Gözde, Can, Rafi. in London: Fatma. in Melbourne: Nermin. Missing: Oğuzhan

At the Bioinformatics and Computational Genomics Group, we develop combinatorial algorithms to analyze genomic sequence data to discover, genotype, and phase genomic variants with a special focus on structural variation. We are also interested in algorithms for de novo genome assembly, pangenomics, and hardware/software co-design to accelerate computational workloads.
